Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

tldr: The app collects nothing. No accounts, no tracking, no ads. The website has an optional email signup if you want updates on what's next. That's it.

Who we are

Pac Math is a free mental math game for kids, available at pacmath.lol. It is an independent app with no corporate parent or data-sharing partners.

Data we collect

None. Pac Math does not collect any personal information. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, no email addresses, and no user profiles.

Data stored on your device

The app saves a small amount of gameplay data locally on your device to make the game work. This includes:

  • High scores and daily streak count
  • Total time played
  • Sound preference (on/off)
  • Chompy's health and last feed time (the in-game pet)

This data never leaves your device. It is not synced, uploaded, or backed up to any server we operate. If you delete the app, this data is deleted with it.

Microphone and speech recognition

Pac Math uses your device's microphone and Apple's built-in speech recognition so kids can say their answers out loud instead of typing them. The app will ask for your permission before accessing the microphone.

Speech is processed on-device by Apple's speech recognition framework. No audio is recorded, stored, or sent to our servers. We do not have servers.

Third-party services

The Pac Math app uses zero third-party services. No analytics (no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no anything). No advertising SDKs. No crash reporting tools. No social media integrations. The app is built entirely with Apple's native frameworks.

Website email signup

The pacmath.lol website has an optional email signup form so we can let you know when new updates ship. This form is powered by Kit (formerly ConvertKit), which stores only the email address you provide. Signup is opt-in, and every email includes an unsubscribe link.

This is the only place anything personal is collected, and it lives on the website — not in the app. If you never visit the website or sign up, no information about you exists anywhere.

Children's privacy

Pac Math is designed to be used by children. Because we collect no personal information of any kind, there is no data to protect, share, or delete. The app contains no ads, no in-app purchases, no links to social media, and no way to communicate with other users.

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations by simply not collecting data in the first place.

Internet connection

Pac Math works entirely offline. The app does not require or use an internet connection to function. No data is transmitted over the network.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the app handles data, we will update this page. Given that the app collects nothing, we don't anticipate changes, but we'll keep this page current regardless.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out at hello@pacmath.lol.
