--- DESCRIPTION (4000 char max) ------------------------------
PacMath is voice-first mental math for kids.

Tap the mic, see a problem, say the answer out loud. Chompy eats the dots and waits for the next one. No typing, no tapping, no stylus, no worksheets — just math you say with your voice.

FEATURES
• Voice answers powered by on-device speech recognition
• Classic mode: 10 problems on your chosen level
• Endless mode: problems that adapt as you improve
• Four difficulty tiers: easy, medium, hard, beast
• Add and subtract (easy), multiply and divide (medium+)
• Daily streaks, high scores, best-answer time, and a full recap of every run
• Customize Chompy with a name and color
• Works offline — no accounts, no ads, no data collection

PRIVACY
PacMath does not collect data. Speech recognition runs entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to a server. Ever. See pacmath.lol/privacy for details.

BUILT FOR PRACTICE
PacMath is built around the idea that saying math out loud is how it sticks. Worksheets and calculators both bypass the part of practice that matters: producing the answer from your head, on the spot, with no buttons in between. PacMath gives kids (and adults) a short, hands-free session that rewards speed, accuracy, and streaks.

Feed Chompy. Get better at math.

--- REVIEW NOTES (for App Review) ----------------------------
PacMath is a voice-first math game. To test: tap the microphone button, then say the answer to the math problem out loud. All speech recognition is on-device (no network required). The app has no accounts, no purchases, no ads, and does not collect any data.

Tip: a physical device is recommended — speech recognition works in Simulator but is more reliable on-device. If you're testing in Simulator and recognition fails, the "skip" button lets you advance past any problem.
